Don’t have a New Year’s Resolution yet? Check out these 9 ways to rejuvenate yourself with humor!

9 Ways to Rejuvenate Yourself with Humor

  1. Tickle your own funny bone … ?
  2. Laugh - whether you want to or not
  3. Sing your gripes away
  4. Observe people
  5. Empty your mind of worries
  6. Create book titles about your predicament
  7. Make weird sounds
  8. Take a class in improvisational comedy
  9. Keep your eyes (and your mind) open for silly solutions

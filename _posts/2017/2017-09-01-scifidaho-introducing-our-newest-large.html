#scifidaho Introducing our newest large collection! This summer, Special Collections received a large personal collection of science fiction materials owned by University of Idaho alumna and long-time Moscow, ID resident Victoria (Vicki) E. Mitchell.

Mitchell (along with her soon-to-be husband Jon Gustafson) was a member of the Palouse Empire Science Fiction Association (PESFA) and a 1978 founding member of MosCon, the annual science fiction convention in Moscow. Vicki organized MosCon for over 20 years and was also a member of local writing groups and workshops including Writer’s Bloc and the Moscow Moffia Writers’ Program. She published several science fiction works, won a national story contest, and her first novel, Enemy Unseen: A Star Trek Novel, spent three weeks on the New York Times Bestseller list in 1990. Mitchell worked as a staff geologist for the Idaho Geological Survey for many years and won the Idaho State Historical Society Esto Perpetua award for promoting and preserving Idaho history in 2006.

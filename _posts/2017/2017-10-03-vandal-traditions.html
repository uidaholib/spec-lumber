Happy Homecoming Vandals!

If you’re a local or coming into town for the festivities, make sure to check out the Vandal Traditions exhibit displayed at Moscow City Hall! No worries if you can’t make it this week, the exhibit will be up until November 24th. Materials on display from Special Collections and Archives, the Leila Old Historic Clothing Collection, and Vandal Athletics.

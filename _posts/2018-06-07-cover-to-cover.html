The University of Idaho Library Special Collections and Archives commemorates GLBT Book Month in June.

As explained by the American Library Association this commemoration is held annually to honor the authors and writing that reflect the lives and experiences of the gay, lesbian, bisexual and transgender community. Included among the Special Collections and Archives book collections is the Stonewall Collection. The Stonewall Collection consists of books proposed for consideration for the American Library Association's Stonewall Book Awards (including winners and honor books) as representative of the stream of publishing representing the gay, lesbian, bisexual and transgender experience. These books are award winners from 2013 that are part of the Stonewall Collection.

The Stonewall Book Awards Collection was presented to Special Collections by Lewis Brian Day.

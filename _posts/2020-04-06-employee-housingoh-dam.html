Welcome back to another week of Oh Dam!, our mini-series on dams in the Pacific Northwest.

Ever wonder where dam construction workers live while these dams are being built? These photos show employee housing around the Grand Coulee Dam. The majority of homes were 2-bedroom and 3-bedroom permanent type residences.

Photos courtesy of PG 94, (Army Corps of Engineers Dam Construction Photographs, 1933-1965), Special Collections and Archives, University of Idaho Library, Moscow, Idaho.

Haven’t written because I / we have been: 

Falling off a chair lift

Lost in a box canyon

Buried in an avalanche

Marooned on a mountain top

Entertaining the Pony Express

Falling off moguls

Hooking giant trout

Trailing an Elk

Climbing the Rockies

Alone in a ghost town

Pushing Powder on the slopes

“Narrow Gauging” it

Singing ‘round a campfire

Dodging Vultures

Sampling “Coors”

This New Year’s Eve, we say goodbye to 2019 and welcome the 2020s. Here is a poem to commemorate this momentous occasion: 

“Nuanced moments filled with anticipation.
Every eye turned to the sky 
Wondering at the possibilities.

Yesteryear has been fulfilled,
Enlightened optimism forthcoming.
All the world gathered to celebrate.
Resolutions fill each mind, humming.
Silent reverie accompanied by boisterous jubilation.

Enjoy this
Visceral 
Evening”

(Poem by Spec Staff)

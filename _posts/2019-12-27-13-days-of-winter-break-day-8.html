This poem is set to the melody of “What Child Is This,” it is called “What Day is This?”

“What day is this?
I’m losing track.
No school or work to keep me.
Netflix, Hulu, and Disney+
I curl on the couch sleeping.

Tumblr posts written past
Post now as they were scheduled
Yet, I think not of these things,
For I am still home sleeping.

What, what day is this?
I’m losing track 
How, how much is left
Of this glorious break

Oh what shall I do until that day
When life returns to normal?
I shall eat leftovers and cookies
Enjoying my last days of freedom. 

What, what day is this?
I’m losing track
How, how much is left
Of this glorious break”

(Poem by Spec Staff)

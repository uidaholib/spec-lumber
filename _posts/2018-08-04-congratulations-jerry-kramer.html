The University of Idaho Library Special Collections and Archives congratulates former University of Idaho Vandal Jerry Kramer on his induction into the Pro-Football Hall of Fame.

Kramer played for the University of Idaho from 1954 to 1957 where he was selected as an All-American. He was drafted by the Green Bay Packers in the fourth-round as the 39th overall pick. He played for Green Bay from 1958 to 1968 where he was a member of five championship teams.Kramer is a member of the Vandal Athletics Hall of Fame, the 50th Anniversary NFL Team and the 50th Anniversary Senior Bowl All-Time Team.

Recent days have seen a shortage of groceries on the shelves of stores, and with the request to maintain social distance we are thinking of this traveling grocery from 1920. Traveling across the Coeur d’Alenes, it brought groceries to all those who lived geographically distant from the luxury of an in-town grocery.

University of Idaho Vandal Volleyball opens the season in Memorial Gym this weekend playing host to the Idaho Volleyball Classic tournament.

The co-North Big Sky Champion Vandals host Santa Clara University, University of New Mexico and Grand Canyon University. Go Vandals!(Images from 1982 Vandal Volleyball schedule, 1988 media guide)

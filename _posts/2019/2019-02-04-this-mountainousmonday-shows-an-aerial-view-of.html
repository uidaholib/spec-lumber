This Mountainous Monday shows an aerial view of Riggins, Idaho, with the Seven Devils Mountains off to the right on the horizon. Riggins, located alongside the Salmon River, falls in between the Hells Canyon National Recreation Area (including the Hells Canyon Wilderness area), the Payette National Forest and the Nez Perce-Clearwater National Forest. Photo taken by Kyle Laughlin, July 24, 1962. (PG 99_367-01)

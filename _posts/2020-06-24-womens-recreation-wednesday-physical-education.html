Welcome back to another week of Women’s Recreation Wednesday, our series covering the Women’s Recreation Association here at the University of Idaho.

This week’s photos are from Women’s Recreation Association Physical Education classes. If you’re interested in learning more about the history of Physical Education classes for women, there’s a great book titled “Active Bodies: A History of Women’s Physical Education in Twentieth-Century America” by Martha H. Verbrugge!

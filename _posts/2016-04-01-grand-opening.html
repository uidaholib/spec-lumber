Today at 2 p.m. PDT is the grand opening and ribbon cutting ceremony for our newly renovated first floor.  10 years in the making, the new first floor will feature a 24-hour welcoming space complete with comfy couches and study space and a cafe.  Also new to the Library is the MILL, or Making, Innovating and Learning Laboratory.  For more information: please visit student newspaper The Argonaut article Turning the page — Library renovations formally unveiled.

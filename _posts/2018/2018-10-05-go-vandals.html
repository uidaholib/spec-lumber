The University of Idaho Vandals travel to Pocatello this weekend to take on cross state and Big Sky Conference rivals the Idaho State Bengals. University of Idaho Library Special Collections and Archives showcases football program covers from three previous meetings between the Vandals and Bengals in 1962, 1965 and 1967.

The Vandals won all three of these contests and we know their gridiron skill will dominate this weekend as well! (Idaho Vandal Football Historical Gameday Program Covers Digital Collection).

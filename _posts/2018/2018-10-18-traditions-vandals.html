It’s Thursday of 2018 Homecoming Week on the University of Idaho campus and one day away from the start of a tradition filled weekend! One of the signature events Friday is the the bonfire, taking place in the Kibbie Dome parking lot at 8:30 following the serpentine. Below, Joe Vandal as well as some die-hard Vandal fans celebrate the 2006 bonfire!

Fireworks sponsored by the Latah Credit Union will follow the bonfire then the TowerLights presentation, sponsored by the Association of Computing Machinery, lights up Theophilus Towers at 9:45. The Homecoming Parade winds down Moscow’s main street at 10 a.m. Saturday morning. The weekend’s main event, the football game between the Idaho Vandals and Southern Utah University, takes place in the Kibbie Dome at 2 p.m.

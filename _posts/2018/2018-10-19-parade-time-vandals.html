The University of Idaho Homecoming Parade takes place Saturday at 10 a.m. in downtown Moscow. Vandal football great and NFL Hall of Fame member Jerry Kramer is the 2018 parade Grand Marshal.

Vandal football against Southern Utah University kicks off in the Kibbie Dome at 2 p.m. Saturday

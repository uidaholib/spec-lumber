Happy birthday to Nancy Wilson!

This American jazz singer was born in 1937, and today we celebrate her 81st birthday!

Nancy Wilson performed at the Lionel Hampton Jazz Festival at the University of Idaho in 2001. The picture above is of Wilson and Lionel Hampton backstage at the Festival in 2001. (UA 2011-015)

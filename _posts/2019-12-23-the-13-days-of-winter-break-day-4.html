What card shall inspire our poetry today, you ask? Why another with a woman and child…

“Is it just me, or is there a theme.
The image I see repeats it seems.
A woman and child.
A woman and child.
A woman and child.
The image below schemes,
To invade all of my dreams. 
Kirkwood loved woman and child in reams.”

(Poem by Spec Staff)

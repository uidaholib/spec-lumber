Happy Birthday Charles Dickens(1812-1870!) The University of Idaho Library Special Collections and Archives remembers Charles Dickens on the 206th anniversary of his birth. Special Collections has two first editions of Dickens’ novel Little Dorrit. Special Collections has the original serial form of the book as it was published in nineteen monthly installments issued from December 1855 to June 1857. Each installment was 32 pages and included two illustrations.

The other first edition of Little Dorrit in University of Idaho Library Special Collections and Archives was published in 1857 and represents the complete work which was printed in one volume after the serialization of the story had concluded. The two works, side by side, show the installments(pictured here on the right) occupied much more room since much of the printing of the work was supported by pages of advertisement included in each story installment.

Little Dorrit was Dickens’ eleventh novel.

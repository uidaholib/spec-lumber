Gotts Point documentary premier tonight at 6:30 at the Kenworthy Theatre in Moscow, ID, brought to you by U of I JAMM Professor Denise Bennett along with the “Idaho Queered” project! Join us and support our local history!

Idaho Queered is an Oral History project led by University of Idaho Professor Denise Bennett. Since 2016, Bennett has traveled across Idaho, recording interviews with members of the LGBTQ community who are willing to speak about their experience. Bennett’s work is being funded by a challenge grant from the National Endowment for the Humanties. In order to access the $30,000 the NEH has made available, we need grants and donations.The “Idaho Queered” project can be supported by donations to the University of Idaho Foundation at 208-885-4000 or online UIdaho Giving by noting “Idaho’s LGBTQ Community Oral History Project” in the designation.

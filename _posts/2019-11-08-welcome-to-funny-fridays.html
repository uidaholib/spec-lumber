Welcome to our latest series, Funny Fridays! Each Friday, we’ll try to supply our followers with humorous materials we’ve stumbled upon in the archives. 

This week’s photo comes from the Tom Kovalicky Papers. Kovalicky was a U.S. Forest Service supervisor at the Nez Perce National Forest in Idaho and had a great sense of humor! Many of the images featured in Funny Fridays will come from his collection! You can view the collection finding aid here.

Photo courtesy of Folder 2, MG 478 (Tom Kovalicky Papers), University of Idaho Library, Special Collections & Archives, Moscow, Idaho.

Today, from 1:00-3:30pm the book artists Peter and Donna Thomas will be giving a papermaking demonstration outside the library. Along with them will be their “Gypsy Wagon” in which they travel across the country. 

After their demonstration, at 4:00pm there will be a reception on the second floor of the library. This reception will take place around the current exhibit A Book’s Nature: Artist Books by Peter and Donna Thomas, with some remarks by the artists beginning at 5:00pm. 

Be sure to stop by to meet these incredible artists. Their work is on display on the second floor of the library through December 10th!

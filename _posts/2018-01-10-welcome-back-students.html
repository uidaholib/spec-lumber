With the start of the Spring Semester, the Library has returned to being open 24/5, starting at 9 am on Sundays then closing at 8 pm on Fridays. Saturdays are reduced hours of 9 am - 6 pm. With the most computers available on campus and comfy couches, the Library is a great place to study or take a nap!

Special Collections’ Reading Room is open Monday - Friday 9 am - 4 pm. For all other hours, please go here.

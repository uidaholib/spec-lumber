On this #PotatoPoTuesday, we have some fun health facts for you courtesy of the Idaho Potato Museum! Did you know that potatoes are roughly 80% water? Or that a cooked 8-ounce potato only contains 100 calories? How about the fact that potatoes are naturally 99.9% fat free? Just a few reasons why potatoes are our favorite vegetable in Idaho!

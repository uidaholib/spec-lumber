Happy 71st Birthday Octavia E. Butler! Born on this day in 1947, Butler was a visionary author, receiving multiple awards such as the Hugo and Nebula for her work and became the first science fiction author to receive a MacArthur Fellowship. These two books come from our Vicki Mitchel Science Fiction Collection.
